Population Migration from State to State

Mouseover to focus on the number of people moving from one state to another.

The thickness of links between states encodes the relative frequency of moves between two states: thicker links represent more people moving.

Links are directed: for example, while 2.2% of rides go from South of Market to Downtown, only 1.2% go in the opposite direction. Links are colored by the more frequent origin.
